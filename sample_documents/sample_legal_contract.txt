EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This Employment Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into on January 15, 2024, between TechCorp Solutions Inc. ("Company") and John Smith ("Employee").

1. POSITION AND DUTIES
Employee shall serve as Senior Software Engineer reporting to the CTO. Primary responsibilities include:
- Designing and developing software applications
- Leading technical architecture decisions
- Mentoring junior developers
- Participating in code reviews

2. COMPENSATION
- Base Salary: $120,000 annually, paid bi-weekly
- Performance Bonus: Up to 15% of base salary based on annual review
- Stock Options: 1,000 shares vesting over 4 years (25% per year)

3. BENEFITS
- Health insurance (medical, dental, vision)
- 401(k) with 4% company match
- 20 days PTO annually
- 10 paid holidays
- Professional development budget: $2,000/year

4. CONFIDENTIALITY
Employee agrees to maintain confidentiality of all proprietary information during and after employment.

5. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate employment with 30 days written notice. Company may terminate immediately for cause.

6. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
All work created during employment belongs to the Company.

This agreement is governed by the laws of California.
